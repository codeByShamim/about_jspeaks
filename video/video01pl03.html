Growth Mindset Vs. Fixed Mindset


Candice Cartner is an Executive Coach and Licensed Therapist dedicated to helping “stuck” business leaders—and others—build clear visions and create mindset shifts to overcome barriers in their lives, work, and relationships.


Utilizing her expertise in Executive Leadership, Organizational Psychology, and Team Development, Candice diagnoses problem areas while supporting the growth of essential skills, strategies, and evaluative tools that lead to measurable evolution in both career and personal outcomes. As a Three Vital Questions Certified Training Facilitator through the Center for The Empowerment Dynamic, she conducts training sessions, workshops, and evidence-based, outcome-driven education for organizations and teams nationwide.


For nearly 14 years, Candice has empowered thousands of clients to achieve their personal and professional goals through her uniquely developed framework for mindset mastery. By aligning their values and vision, she helps them reach beyond the potential they believed they had.


Candice holds an MA in Clinical Mental Health Counseling and a BS in Sociology/Psychology with concentrations in Family, Race, and Minority Relations. She owns a private practice as a Clinical Therapist and operates an Executive Coaching Firm. Residing in North Carolina, she enjoys life with her husband and their two small but mighty children.
