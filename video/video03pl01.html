Introducing Shining Light In Darkness


Shining Light in Darkness is on a mission to be a trusted provider of support, skills, and resources for underserved victims of sexual assault (SA) and domestic violence (DV), aiming to help end violence for all. This organization has evolved into a community of compassionate individuals, united in creating a world free from sexual assault and domestic violence. Through a comprehensive program portfolio that encompasses healing, education, and advocacy, Shining Light in Darkness is pioneering innovative approaches to trauma treatment, shifting public perceptions, and responses to ensure fairness for survivors globally.


Founders ED Dudley and Florida DeVaul Dudley emphasize empathy, empowerment, and family, believing that every survivor of sexual assault and domestic violence is worthy of hope and healing. Their work is driving a transformation in how society views and responds to issues related to sexual assault, domestic violence, and teen dating.
