Always Do The Right Thing


Think of a time you acted on impulse. How did that work out for you? Do you think that was right thing to do? Whenever you have the urge to fight fire with fire please turn your focus to God - he will never forsake you.

"Jesus I can't do this anymore. I am done trying to solve thing on my own. I need you now. I am choosing to trust you. I am trusting you more than I trust myself. I am choosing to trust you more than any scheme or worldly solution. Although this is not what I want to do and quite frankly this is not what I am comfortable doing, I surrender to you. I want you to solve my problems rather than me solve my problems for myself. I want your powers to work in my circumstance. I want your love to flow through me rather than passivity and aggression.

I know I won't be perfect at this but I am going to try. I am going to try to try everyday. I believe that you will give me what I need - to surrender, to love again, to rest my heart. Because you Lord have the power to turn my unfortunate circumstances to endless blessings - because that is the kind of God are. Amen."
