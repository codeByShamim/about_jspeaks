Meet JSpeaks

Host & Director

My name is John Blakley, I am the founder, host and director of JSpeaks. The purpose of my channel is to have real conversations with real people in order to share content to the world that is positive, motivational, educational and informational. I believe that there are educated, motivated and all around good people everywhere who if they had the opportunity would love to impart their knowledge, interests, skills and stories for the greater good of the world. My charge is to try to find those people and use my platform to promote them, their practice, nonprofit and business all the while leaving a positive impact on the world.

Mission

To connect, educate, and empower the world one spotlight at a time.

Vision

To leave a lasting impact on the world that challenges people to think, consider, and better themselves.
